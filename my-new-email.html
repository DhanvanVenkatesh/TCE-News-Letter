Editor's Desk

We are glad to share this issue of the Alumni e-Newsletter with you all. The period between July-September 2021 has been so exciting with the college re-opening for students and everyone getting back to business. Alumni engagement at our institution continues to reach newer heights. Over 500 students have enrolled for getting mentorship under alumni and 180 alumni have volunteered to be mentors. The golden jubilee reunion of 1970 batch alumni happened in blended mode with some alumni attending the event in person while others joined virtually. Many alumni have also been involved in academic activities such as curriculum review, Technical talks and Project reviewers. In this issue, we have covered all major alumni engagements that happened between July-September 2021. We also take immense pleasure in giving special mention to some of our alumni, who have won laurels for their professional achievements. We hope this e-Newsletter would be an effective instrument of communication and positively engage you with the developments at our institution. Your suggestions and feedback are highly appreciated.

You may reach us through: alumni@tce.edu

Golden Jubilee Reunion

1970 Batch - Reunion

On September 27, 2021, the golden jubilee reunion of the 1970 class was held at TCE's KS auditorium. 15 alumni from the 1970 class attended the reunion in person, while many more participated digitally. With his welcome remarks, Dr.K.Ramalingam  led off the programme. In his welcome presentation, Mr.G.Thiagarajan outlined the reunion's goals and objectives. Mr.N.Ramkumar, president of the TCE Alumni Association, gave a talk about the organisation. Dr.V Abhaikumar, Director, Former Principal Dr. S. Mercy Shaline and Principal(i/c) Dr. M PalaninathaRaja were honoured by the alumni who attended the occasion.

Mr. K Hari Thiagarajan, our Trustee, delivered the Presidential address and handed out the souvenir. Dr. V Abhaikumar presented his address after releasing the souvenir film clip. Alumni from the 1970 class offered a donation of Rs.2 lakhs. Dr.M. PalaninathaRaja and Dr.S.Mercy Shalinie provided keynote presentations. Mr. NS Srinivasan spoke about souvenirs in his address. The programme facilitators were Mr. S.Balakrishnan,

Mr. D.V.Krishnan, and Mr. A.Gurumohan from the 1970 batch, who delivered speeches during the occasion. Mr.RM Subbiah addressed the gathering as Chief Co-ordinator. Dr.K.V Uma, College level Co-ordinator for Alumni Relations, gave a brief presentation on alma connect, a platform for alumni engagement. The vote of thanks was offered by Mr. MS Ramanujam. The atmosphere was filled with joy and thankfulness.
